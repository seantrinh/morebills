MoreBills

We're here to save your wallet

We created this web app to help college students budget. The main features of the app are the bank account, the progress chart, the goal list, and the bank meter. You enter your initial funds into your bank account. From there you are able to add or subtract any amount of money from your account. Enter values into the goals list and track your progress.
